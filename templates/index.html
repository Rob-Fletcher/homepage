Experience

Research Assistant - ATLAS Experiment

University of Pennsylvania

Using some of the largest data sets in the world, I worked with teams of physicists in the search for new, lighter versions of the Higgs Boson. In the ATLAS datasets, signals are burried under large backgrounds so it is essential to model and understand these backgrounds well. I have worked on a new method for background modeling using Gaussian Process Regression to make a prediction on noisey data.
